Introduction

Hexmolt ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process your information through our website, application, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using Hexmolt, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect information such as:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile information

Files and Content: Any files, documents, or content you upload to the Service are stored on our servers. We do not access or scan the contents of your files except as necessary to:

  • Provide the Service
  • Maintain security
  • Comply with legal obligations

Communication: When you contact us for support or feedback, we collect the information you provide in your messages, including your contact information.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data: We automatically collect certain information about how you interact with the Service, including:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Upload and download activities

Performance Data: We collect information about Service performance and technical metrics to improve reliability and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To create, maintain, and improve your account and provide the Service
  • Communication: To send you updates, notifications, and support responses
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, security, and technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and legal processes
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our Service to improve it
  • Personalization: To customize your experience and provide relevant content

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers: We may share information with vendors and service providers who perform services on our behalf, including:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Payment processors
  • Analytics providers
  • Customer support tools

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, legal process, or government request, including:

  • Subpoenas or court orders
  • Government or regulatory requests
  • Investigations of potential violations

Business Transfers: If Hexmolt is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

3.2 User-Initiated Sharing

When you choose to share files or folders with others through the Service, those individuals will have access to the shared content according to the permissions you set.

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • End-to-end encryption for file transfers
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive data
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Specifically:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward
  • User Files: Retained until you delete them or close your account
  • Usage Logs: Retained for up to 90 days for security and analytics purposes
  • Legal Hold: Information may be retained longer if required by law

When you delete your account, we will remove your personal information from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to request access to your personal information and receive a copy in a portable format. Contact us at abhisingh1000500@gmail.com to make such a request.

6.2 Correction and Deletion

You may correct inaccurate information or request deletion of your account and associated personal data. Note that deletion of files through the Service removes them from your account.

6.3 Cookies and Tracking

6.4 Communications

You may opt out of receiving promotional communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or contacting us directly.
